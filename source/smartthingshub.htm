SmartThings Hub

Devices > Sources

Represents a connection to a SmartThings hub.

Properties

Property Description
BaseURL The base URL of the Home Remote SmartApp.
Endpoint The endpoint URL of the Home Remote SmartApp. (See Remarks)
Name The identifying name of the object.
RefreshRate The rate in milliseconds that the connection should refresh its content.

Remarks

The Home Remote app requires the Home Remote SmartApp be installed on your SmartThings hub. Follow these steps to add a SmartThings connection:

  1. Open the SmartThings Hub dialog in the Home Remote Designer
  2. Log into https://graph.api.smartthings.com/ from any web browser
  3. Click "My SmartApps"
  4. Click "New SmartApp"
  5. Click "From Code"
  6. Copy and paste the code from Home Remote for SmartThings Source
  7. Click "Create"
  8. Click "App Settings"
  9. Click "OAuth"
  10. Click "Enable OAuth in Smart App"
  11. Copy and paste the "OAuth Client ID" into the "OAuth Client ID" entry on the SmartThings Hub dialog
  12. Copy and paste the "OAuth Client Secret" into the "OAuth Client Secret" entry on the SmartThings Hub dialog
  13. Click "Update"
  14. Click "Code"
  15. Select your location and click "Set Location"
  16. Click "Publish"
  17. Click "For Me"
  18. Click the Search button on the SmartThings Hub dialog.
  19. Log into your SmartThings account.
  20. Check the devices you wish to give the Home Remote app access to control and monitor.
  21. Click "Authorize"
  22. Click the OK button on the SmartThings Hub dialog.
